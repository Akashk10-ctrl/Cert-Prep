Welcome to THE Music Store

Here at THE Music Store, we have the finest selection of instruments and accessories. Whether you want a guitar, piano, drums, ukulele, trombone, or even a kazoo, you've come to the right place.

We have music books, sheet music, and other accessories available too.

We currently offer courses for:

  • Guitar
  • Piano
  • Drums

Once a month, we offer a free introductory course. To sign up:

  1. Join our music club
  2. Sign up for event emails
  3. Enroll in a course

We’re open every day. Hours vary by season, so check back often.
